Welcome to the Arena, Friend Warrior!

It is 10,000 years from now. Civilization lies ruined, ravished by a zombie apocalypse of man's own creation.

The only thing that can save mankind from its folly now lies deep in the fiendish house of traps known as the Cave of Terrors...

Instructions

The goal of the game is to claim the treasure at the bottom of the Cave of Terrors.

The cave of terrors has 100 levels.

Once you have beaten a level of the Cave of Terrors, you can always start from any level at that level or below.

If you die, you lose half your gold, and half your experience.

If you can beat a level higher than the one at which you died, without dying again, your gold and experience will be returned to you.

Purchase armor and weapons from the store.

More powerful weapons and armor will become available as you unlock more levels of the cave.

Other adventurers may be persuaded to join your party. If your party members die, they are gone forever.

Other adventurers cannot be controlled directly, but you can customize their behaviour in the Party menu.
