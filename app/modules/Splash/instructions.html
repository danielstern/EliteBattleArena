Instructions

The goal of the game is to claim the treasure at the bottom of the Cave of Terrors.

The cave of terrors has 9 levels.

Once you have beaten a level of the Cave of Terrors, you can always start from any level at that level or below.

If you die, you lose half your gold.

Purchase armor and weapons from the store.

You can sell items in your possession in your inventory screen.

Battle

During battle you must kill your opponents.

Press attack to attack your opponents.

Healing restores a substantial amount of life. You can only heal once per battle.
